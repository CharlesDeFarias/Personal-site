Charles De Farias

Brazilian-American Full Stack software developer with a particular love for ES6 & exceeding expectations

In this sea of engineers, what makes me such a catch?

Curious, effecient & resourceful coder

I was blessed with the immense privilige of having participated in the Resilient Coders' bootcamp and having a logical way of thinking since childhood. The pleasure center in my brain that used to be satisfied by crosswords and video games are now scratched silly by creating something new, learning another programming language or gaining greater mastery in the ones I already know. I am perpetually dedicated to making my code better, smarter and shorter. I strive to impress not just my superiors, but also myself. The only thing better than figuring out why something broke is figuring out how to make it better.

I have a particular passion for the endless potential that comes from the simple combination of HTML5, CSS and ES6. I'm also experienced with MongoDB, Node & React. I plan on becoming much more familiar with React due to its versatility.

Positive, authentic & humorous office presence

I am consistently dedicated to improving the day of as many people as possible. While I can be oddly cold and calculative when it comes to making decisions, I am extremely invested in the happiness & success of those around me. Since college, I have learned to create a community wherever I land. I've learned how to form a bond with just about anyone and have that bond be a positive element in both our lives. I'm dedicated you creating, fostering and maintaining positive & uplifting relationships

Charismatic, enthusiastic and loyal brand ambassador

I was raised to always acknowledge, respect & praise my privilige. I know that even what I achieve through my hard work and intellect is a result of the luck I had in being born to the family and time I was born into. For this reason, I always give my all to any opportunity granted to me. Whether that be a bootcamp like the glorious Resilient Coders or an employer that values me, I will always do my best to make sure both they and the world know how much I appreciate them.
